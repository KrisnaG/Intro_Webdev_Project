ABOUT

By selecting a particular Queensland animal species by class and family, information is provided about the species scientific names, alternate names, pest information and images. Note that the information is sourced externally, therefore, not all species listed are provided with information and/or images.

All data was provided by the Queensland government from its WildNet database, which has been approved for public use. More information on the WildNet database can be found here.
